Life At RCS

Building a vibrant community of creative and accomplished people

Entrepreneurship programme

We believe that education goes beyond what happens in the classroom only, being the reason why we set up an entrepreneurship programme in our school. It is a programme that has been equipping the learners the needed skills to be self-employed, regardless of their profession in the future and even now.

Sport & Games

Royal Creascent School believes that physical exercise promotes healthy living and mental alertness, this is why sporting activities are made compulsory for every child in the school. Every student is encouraged to participate in at least one sport, and trainers/coaches are always on ground to guide and to train them.

Hostel Activities

RICO is a residential institution with a regular day-school educational system. Therefore special and individual attention is given to the boarders for the remaining part of the day. All the boys and girls are involved in variety of cognitive and co native activities for a holistic development.

Why Choose Us?

A choice that makes the difference.

Our Curriculum

Our curriculum and programs are differentiated based on students' needs. Our supportive learning environment enhances each unique student’s skill set by guiding them through hands-on academic activities. Our Learning Support Team provides each student with opportunity to grow in areas of strength and provide hands on engagement and learning so all students can reach their full potential.

Our Teachers

All of our highly trained teachers possess many years of educational experience paired with a passion for growing and nurturing our students. Though the teachers lead the way, we also foster self-motivation, critical thinking, and accountability in our students. Our small class sizes deliver a very personalized and engaging learning experience where every student is valued and recognized.

Experienced Leadership

Leading a large, busy school such as Royal Creasscent School and sustaining and building on its high levels of success is a huge responsibility. It relies upon a passionate and dedicated team of senior staff with vision who are driven by a commitment to develop students academically, physically, socially and spiritually.

Collaborative Approach

Royal Creasscent School's collaborative approach gives students the opportunity to play an active role in their education and take ownership of their futures. At all ages, students balance engaging independent study with stimulating group work and team collaboration.

Expanding Horizons

The academy is committed to the all-round development of your child’s talents and interests. The extra-curricular programs vary and exciting, challenging and stretching, allowing children the chance to gain new experiences or deepen existing skills outside the four walls of the classroom.

Real World Application

Real-world application is critical. Our programs are developed not only to promote student success in the classroom, but far beyond it and for years to come. Henderson International students are equipped with life tools. Students are confident, inspired and prepared.
