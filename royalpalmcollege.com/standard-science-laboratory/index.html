EQUIPPED SCIENCE LABORATORY

  • Posted by Royal Palm College
  • Categories Gallery, Lab
  • Date September 1, 2022

At RCS we ensure the best facilities are provided for teaching and learning, since our management team recognize that each child has the potential to exceed expectation, the students are then encouraged to question and apply their knowledge, think creatively, be accommodating and ponder over their learning to achieve even better result.

Our science Laboratory is standard for teaching of sciences. We have a standard laboratories for all science subjects, like Physics, Chemistry, Biology etc. It is our happiness to inform you that our pupils/students achieve excellent results in both internal and external examinations. Students are also exposed to various co-curricular programmes which include drama, sport, music and many other areas. It is our expectation that our children (i.e. pupils/students) will benefit from this exciting provision.
